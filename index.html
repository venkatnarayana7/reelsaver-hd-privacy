Privacy Policy

ReelSaver HD  ·  Last updated: May 2025

ReelSaver HD ("we", "us", "our") is a free Android application that lets users download publicly available Instagram Reels and Posts, and extract audio from them. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, location, etc.).

The app processes Instagram URLs that you paste — these URLs are used solely to fetch the media file from Instagram's public CDN and are never sent to our servers (we have none). All processing happens entirely on your device.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

ReelSaver HD uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve personalised ads, including:

  • Device advertising ID (GAID / IDFA)
  • Device type, OS version, language, and time zone
  • IP address (used for approximate location)
  • App usage data and ad interaction data

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own Privacy Policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by visiting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or Opt out of Ads Personalisation (earlier versions).

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party SDK whose privacy practices are governed by their respective policies:

  • Google AdMob – AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services – Google Privacy Policy

We do not use analytics SDKs, crash-reporting tools, or any other data-collection service beyond AdMob.

4. Permissions Used

  • INTERNET – Required to fetch video/audio files from Instagram's public CDN and to load advertisements.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 8 only) – Required to save downloaded files to the Movies or Music folder on your device.

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive permission.

5. Downloaded Content

Files downloaded through ReelSaver HD are saved directly to your device's local storage (Movies/ReelSaverHD or Music/ReelSaverHD). We have no access to these files. You are solely responsible for ensuring that your use of downloaded content complies with Instagram's Terms of Use and applicable copyright laws. Download only content that you own or have permission to save.

6. Children's Privacy

ReelSaver HD is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data on our end. AdMob's data retention is governed by Google's own policies. Downloaded media files remain on your device until you choose to delete them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
